1. What Soilify does

It answers one question for you every day: should I water, or not?

A small sensor sits in your soil and measures how wet or dry it is, every 15 minutes, day and night. That reading goes to your phone. Soilify combines it with your local weather and tells you plainly: water today, or you're good, wait.

Instead of guessing or checking by hand, you glance at your phone. You save water, and your crops never get too dry without you knowing.

2. What's in your kit

The field sensorA small weatherproof unit you push into the soil. Battery powered — lasts months. This is what reads your dirt.
The gatewayA small box that stays indoors near your WiFi router, plugged into power. It has a little screen. It listens to the sensor and sends the readings to the internet.
Cables & guideA USB power cable for the gateway and this manual.
Why two pieces? The sensor lives outside in your field and runs on battery, so it uses radio (not WiFi) to save power. The gateway sits inside on WiFi and passes the messages along. You set up the gateway once; the sensors just work.

3. Setting it up

About 10 minutes. You only do this once.

First, create your account

  1. On your phone, go to soilifylabs.com.
  2. Tap Sign in and choose your Google account.
  3. Follow the short setup: name your farm, set your location, and choose when you want alerts.
  4. At the end you'll see a 6-digit pairing code. Keep that screen open — you'll need it in a moment. (It also lives in Settings → Gateway pairing code.)

Then, set up the gateway

1
Plug in the gatewayPut it indoors near your WiFi router and connect the power cable. The screen lights up.
2
Connect your phone to itIn your phone's WiFi settings, join the network named "Soilify-Setup". A setup page opens by itself — like the WiFi login at a hotel.
3
Enter your detailsPick your home WiFi, type its password, and type the 6-digit code from the app. Tap Save.
4
Put the sensor in your soilPush the field sensor into the ground where you want to measure. Turn it on. Within 15 minutes it shows up in your app on its own.
The gateway's screen shows your sensor's moisture, a bar, and a big WATER warning if the soil gets too dry. If you ever move house or change WiFi, hold the PRG button and tap RST to open setup again.

4. Reading your app

Here's what each part of the screen means.

The top banner — your answerThe big band at the top says "Water today" (and how much) or "You're good — no watering needed." This is the one thing most people check.
The moisture dialShows how wet your soil is right now, 0–100%. Red = dry, green = just right, blue = very wet. It updates on its own every 15 minutes.
Plant healthTap it to get an AI check of your soil trend, or snap a photo of your crops and it tells you what it sees.
Watering forecastExplains today's advice using the weather — how much water your crop needs versus how much rain fell.
ChartsSee how your soil moisture has moved over the day and week, so you know if your watering is working.
SettingsChange your farm name, location, crop type, alert number, and see your pairing code and sensors.

5. Getting text alerts

In Settings, add your mobile number and set the "alert threshold" — the dryness level that triggers a warning. When your soil drops below it, Soilify sends you a text so you can act. You won't get spammed; there's a quiet period between alerts.

6. Sharing with family

In Settings → Share farm access, tap to create a link. Send it to family or a helper. When they open it and sign in, they can see your farm's live dashboard, charts, and alerts — but only you, the owner, can change settings.

7. When something's wrong

The app says "Offline." No reading has come in for a while. Check the gateway has power and your WiFi is working. If the gateway is fine, the sensor may be too far away or its battery is low.
No readings are showing up. Make sure the gateway screen isn't on the setup screen (that means it hasn't connected). Re-do the gateway setup if needed. The sensor only sends every 15 minutes, so give it time.
The gateway screen is blank. Unplug it, wait 5 seconds, plug it back in.
The watering advice won't load. Make sure you set your farm location in Settings — the weather math needs it.
"Soilify-Setup" WiFi doesn't appear. The gateway only shows it when it isn't set up yet, or when you hold PRG and tap RST.

8. Caring for the hardware

  • Keep the gateway indoors and dry. It is not weatherproof.
  • The field sensor handles rain, but keep the electronics end above the mud line.
  • Charge or replace the sensor battery if the app shows it going offline often.
  • Clean the metal probe gently if it gets caked; don't scrub the electronics.
